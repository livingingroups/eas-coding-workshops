# Tech Tips Buffet: 50 Tips in 50 Mins

## Goals
- Everyone learns something interesting.
- Everyone who codes on a regular basis, learns something useful.
- Every tip is at least interesting to at least one person.
- Cover topics roughly in proportion to how many people they are relevant to.
- You can tell whether a tip is relevant to you, and if so in what situation you would use it.
- If it is relevant, you know where to start in learning how to implement the concept.

## Anti goals
- Proscriptivism
- Any one person getting something out of every tip.
- Give enough background on each tip so that people who are not in the situation where it's needed will understand it.
- 1min tip telling you everything you need to know to implement the concept without futhur help or reading
